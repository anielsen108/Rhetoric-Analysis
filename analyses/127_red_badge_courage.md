## PASSAGE

[L1] The cold passed reluctantly from the earth, and the retiring fogs revealed an army stretched out on the hills, resting. As the landscape changed from brown to green, the army awakened, and began to tremble with eagerness at the noise of rumors.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Personification** (per-sah-nuh-fuh-KAY-shun)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Human qualities to non-human.
**Evidence:** "passed reluctantly"
**Effect:** Makes nature and army into sentient beings with emotions.
**Near-miss:** Prosopopoeia goes further and gives the thing a speaking voice. Pathetic fallacy is personification of nature specifically, colored by the observer's emotion.

### **Metaphor** (MET-uh-for)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Implicit comparison.
**Evidence:** "The cold passed reluctantly from the earth"
**Effect:** Transforms military unit into single living entity.
**Near-miss:** Simile flags the comparison with like/as, keeping the two things separate. A metaphor sustained across a passage becomes extended metaphor or allegory; a strained, category-breaking one is catachresis.

### **Synecdoche** (sin-EK-duh-kee)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Part for whole.
**Evidence:** "army"
**Effect:** Emphasizes collective over individual experience.
**Near-miss:** A subtype of metonymy: synecdoche requires the part/whole relation specifically, not just any association.

---

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Parataxis** (pair-uh-TAK-sis / ˌpærəˈtæksɪs)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Independent units placed beside one another with minimal explicit hierarchy.
**Evidence:** "The cold passed reluctantly from the earth, and the retiring fogs revealed an army stretched out on the hills, resting. As the landscape changed from brown to green, the army awakened, and began to tremble with eagerness at the noise of rumors."
**Effect:** The adjacent statements make the reader infer their relation, giving the transition pressure without over-explaining it.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

**Evidence:** "All happy families are alike"

**Near-miss:** Oxymoron is paradox compressed to a two-word collision ('living death'); antithesis balances opposites without asserting both are true.

**Lines:** L1

**Evidence:** "All happy families are alike; each"
**Effect:** Establishes narrative authority; claims philosophical insight.

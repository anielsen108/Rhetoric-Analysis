**Occasion/Pressure:** This lyric passage must establish its controlling situation while making a small excerpt bear the work's larger pressure.

## PASSAGE

[L1] Now I am ready to tell how bodies are changed
[L2] Into different bodies.
[L3] I summon the supernatural beings
[L4] who first contrived
[L5] The transformations in the stuff of life.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Invocation** (in-voh-KAY-shun)
**Lines:** L3
**Definition:** Calling upon higher powers for inspiration.
**Evidence:** "I summon the supernatural beings"
**Effect:** Claims divine authority for the narrative; follows epic convention.
**Near-miss:** Apostrophe is any address to the absent; invocation specifically petitions for aid at a threshold.

### **Metalepsis** (met-uh-LEP-sis)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Reference to creative process itself.
**Evidence:** "Now I am ready to tell"
**Effect:** Makes storytelling itself part of the story; self-reflexive.
**Near-miss:** Simple metonymy makes one associative hop; metalepsis needs at least two. The narrative sense (frame-breaking) is the common modern usage.

### **Synecdoche** (sin-EK-duh-kee)
**Lines:** L5
**Definition:** Part for whole or whole for part.
**Evidence:** "stuff of life"
**Effect:** Makes transformations about physical essence, not just appearance.
**Near-miss:** A subtype of metonymy: synecdoche requires the part/whole relation specifically, not just any association.

---

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Parataxis** (pair-uh-TAK-sis / ˌpærəˈtæksɪs)
**Lines:** L1–5
**Definition:** Independent units placed beside one another with minimal explicit hierarchy.
**Evidence:** "Now I am ready to tell how bodies are changed Into different bodies. I summon the supernatural beings who first contrived The transformations in the stuff of life."
**Effect:** The adjacent statements make the reader infer their relation, giving the transition pressure without over-explaining it.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

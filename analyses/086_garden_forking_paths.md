## PASSAGE

[L1] I thought of a labyrinth of labyrinths, of one sinuous spreading labyrinth that would encompass the past and the future and in some way involve the stars.

**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Exaggeration.
**Evidence:** "future and"
**Effect:** Makes maze cosmic, infinite.
**Near-miss:** Its opposite pole is understatement/litotes. When exaggeration collapses into the trivial for comic effect, that's bathos.

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Anaphora** (uh-NAF-or-uh)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Repetition at beginning of phrases.
**Evidence:** "of a labyrinth"
**Effect:** Creates recursive structure mirroring labyrinth concept.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

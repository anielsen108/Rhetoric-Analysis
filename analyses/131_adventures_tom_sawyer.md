## PASSAGE

[L1] Tom appeared on the sidewalk with a bucket of whitewash and a long-handled brush. He surveyed the fence, and all gladness left him and a deep melancholy settled down upon his spirit. Thirty yards of board fence nine feet high. Life to him seemed hollow, and existence but a burden.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Personification** (per-sah-nuh-fuh-KAY-shun)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Human qualities to abstractions.
**Evidence:** "melancholy settled down"
**Effect:** Makes emotions into active agents that come and go.
**Near-miss:** Prosopopoeia goes further and gives the thing a speaking voice. Pathetic fallacy is personification of nature specifically, colored by the observer's emotion.

### **Hyperbole** (hy-PER-buh-lee)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Exaggeration.
**Evidence:** "seemed hollow, and existence but a burden"
**Effect:** Inflates boyhood chore into existential crisis.
**Near-miss:** Its opposite pole is understatement/litotes. When exaggeration collapses into the trivial for comic effect, that's bathos.

### **Bathos** (BAY-thahs)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Descent from elevated to trivial.
**Evidence:** "Tom appeared on the sidewalk with a bucket of whitewash and a"
**Effect:** Creates humor through disproportion.
**Near-miss:** Pathos moves; bathos drops. Anticlimax is the structural name for the same descent.

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Polysyndeton** (pah-lee-SIN-duh-ton / ˌpɒlɪˈsɪndətɒn)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Deliberate multiplication of conjunctions.
**Evidence:** "Tom appeared on the sidewalk with a bucket of whitewash and a long-handled brush. He surveyed the fence, and all gladness left him and a deep melancholy settled down upon his spirit. Thirty yards of board fence nine feet high. Life to him seemed hollow, and existence but a burden."
**Effect:** The repeated conjunctions keep each item audible, turning sequence into accumulation rather than a compressed list.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

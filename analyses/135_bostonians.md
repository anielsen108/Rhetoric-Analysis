## PASSAGE

[L1] Olive Chancellor was unmarried by every implication of her being. She was a spinster as Shelley was a lyric poet, or as the month of August is sultry.

**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Apparent contradiction.
**Evidence:** "by every implication of her being"
**Effect:** Makes singleness ontological, not circumstantial.
**Near-miss:** Oxymoron is paradox compressed to a two-word collision ('living death'); antithesis balances opposites without asserting both are true.

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Anaphora** (uh-NAF-or-uh)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Repetition at beginning.
**Evidence:** "or as"
**Effect:** Accumulates comparisons establishing essence.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

## PASSAGE

[L1] I was born in the city of Bombay... once upon a time. No, that won't do, there's no getting away from the date: I was born in Doctor Narlikar's Nursing Home on August 15th, 1947. And the time? The time matters, too. Well then: at night. No, it's important to be more... On the stroke of midnight, as a matter of fact.

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Anaphora** (uh-NAF-or-uh)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Repetition at beginning.
**Evidence:** "I was born"
**Effect:** Insists on birth narrative despite interruptions.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

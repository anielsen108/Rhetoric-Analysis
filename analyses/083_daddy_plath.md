# Passage 083: "Daddy"

**Sylvia Plath** · *Ariel* · 1965 | Selected passage

**Occasion/Pressure:** This lyric passage must establish its controlling situation while making a small excerpt bear the work's larger pressure.
**Persona/Stance:** A first-person voice controls what the reader can know and how quickly the scene or proposition comes into focus.
**Thesis of Effect:** Through emphatic negation, present perfect duration, and forced rhyme, Plath creates syntax of childlike confrontation—grammar that speaks trauma through nursery-rhyme rhythms.

---

## PASSAGE

[L1] You do not do, you do not do
[L2] Any more, black shoe
[L3] In which I have lived like a foot
[L4] For thirty years, poor and white,
[L5] Barely daring to breathe or Achoo.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Simile** (SIM-ih-lee)
**Lines:** L3
**Definition:** Explicit comparison.
**Evidence:** "lived like a foot"
**Effect:** Renders speaker trapped, controlled by the shoe.
**Near-miss:** Drop the 'like' and it becomes metaphor. An epic simile extends the comparison over many lines with its own internal scene.

### **Slant Rhyme** (SLANT-RYME)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Approximate rhyme.
**Evidence:** "white, Barely daring to breathe or Achoo"
**Effect:** Creates nursery-rhyme effect; makes horror childlike.
**Near-miss:** The label depends on the relation between “white, Barely daring to breathe or Achoo” and the surrounding sentence, not on the phrase in isolation.

### **Hyperbole** (hy-PER-buh-lee)
**Lines:** L5
**Definition:** Exaggeration.
**Evidence:** "Barely daring to breathe"
**Effect:** Intensifies suffocation of patriarchal oppression.
**Near-miss:** Its opposite pole is understatement/litotes. When exaggeration collapses into the trivial for comic effect, that's bathos.

---

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Epizeuxis** (eh-pih-ZOOK-sis)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Immediate repetition.
**Evidence:** "You do not do, you do not do"
**Effect:** Creates childlike sing-song; emphasizes finality.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

## PASSAGE

[L1] The scene by the fountain, its air of ugly threat, and at the heart of it, her older sister, her back arched, her arm upraised, made Briony's writing ambitions, which were to be a post office clerk, seem childish.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Prolepsis** (proh-LEP-sis)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Anticipation of objection.
**Evidence:** "which were to be a post office clerk"
**Effect:** Retrospectively mocks earlier limited ambition.
**Near-miss:** Analepsis looks backward (flashback); prolepsis looks forward. In rhetoric, procatalepsis is the objection-answering subtype.

**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Mixing sensory modes.
**Evidence:** "buckle of noise"
**Effect:** Blends touch and sound into unified sensation.
**Near-miss:** The label depends on the relation between “buckle of noise” and the surrounding sentence, not on the phrase in isolation.

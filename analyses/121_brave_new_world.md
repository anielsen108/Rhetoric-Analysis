## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Zeugma** (ZOOG-muh)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** One verb governing different objects.
**Evidence:** "mocked the gigantic letters in which they were written"
**Effect:** Creates semantic complexity; form contradicts content.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

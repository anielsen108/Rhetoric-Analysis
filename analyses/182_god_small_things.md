## PASSAGE

[L1] May in Ayemenem is a hot, brooding month. The days are long and humid. The river shrinks and black crows gorge on bright mangoes in still, dustgreen trees. Red bananas ripen. Jackfruits burst.

---

## A) RHETORICAL TROPES USED

### **Personification** (per-sah-nuh-fuh-KAY-shun)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Human qualities to abstractions.
**Evidence:** "burst"
**Effect:** Makes landscape emotionally charged, volatile.
**Near-miss:** Prosopopoeia goes further and gives the thing a speaking voice. Pathetic fallacy is personification of nature specifically, colored by the observer's emotion.

### **Synesthesia** (sin-es-THEE-zhuh)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Mixing sensory modes.
**Evidence:** "dustgreen"
**Effect:** Fuses visual and tactile into compound sensation.
**Near-miss:** The label depends on the relation between “dustgreen” and the surrounding sentence, not on the phrase in isolation.

---

## B) RHETORICAL SCHEMES USED

### **Asyndeton** (uh-SIN-duh-tahn)
**Lines:** L1
**Definition:** Omission of conjunctions.
**Evidence:** "Jackfruits burst"
**Effect:** Creates staccato rhythm of accumulated observations.
**Risk:** Repeated mechanically, this pattern could become mannered; here its local placement keeps it functional.

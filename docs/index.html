Rhetoric

Learn to read the choices.
Then learn to make them.

Rhetoric is both a way of seeing language and a craft practiced in real time. This site joins those two kinds of learning: close analysis of accomplished writing, followed by structured exercises that put its techniques in your voice.

One craft, two directions

Analysis sharpens perception. Practice turns perception into choice.

You can begin on either side. Move from a passage to a drill when you want to embody a technique; return from a drill to the Reader when you want to hear how accomplished writers solve the same problem.

Stylistic Effect

Through simple syntax, economical dialogue, and understated language, Fugard creates devastating revelation. The form performs the content: apartheid makes even simple human kindness impossible. The stripped syntax—no elaboration, no rhetoric, just plain statement—makes the tragedy more powerful. This is grammar as heartbreak: syntax so simple it seems transparent, but carrying enormous weight. The very plainness of "making me feel good" as something now prohibited exposes the system's cruelty through understated, compressed grammar.

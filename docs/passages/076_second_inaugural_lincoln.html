Syntax

Triple Prepositional Opening

"With malice toward none, with charity for all, with firmness in the right"—three parallel prepositional phrases beginning with "with," each specifying an attitude or principle.

Subordinate Qualification

"as God gives us to see the right"—subordinate clause qualifying "firmness in the right." The syntax acknowledges human fallibility even while asserting moral certainty.

Cohortative Imperative

"let us strive on"—first person plural imperative. Syntax includes speaker in command, making this collective call to action.

Infinitive of Purpose

"to finish the work we are in"—infinitive phrase expressing goal. The relative clause "we are in" suggests ongoing work, not yet complete.

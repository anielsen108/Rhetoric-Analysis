Stylistic Effect

Through intransitive came-to-us arrival, instrumental by-sea means, definite child-Clara attribution, possessive delicate-calligraphy manner, and embedded narration structure, Allende creates syntax of magical chronicle—grammar that makes dog's arrival mythic through maritime journey while grounding fantastic in specific narrator's voice, characterized by youth and refined penmanship.

Stylistic Effect

Through personifying adverbs, participial revelation, collective singular subject, and infinitive trembling, Crane creates syntax of impressionistic naturalism—grammar that dissolves individual soldiers into atmospheric collective organism responding to environment.

Information flow: The opening establishes “The cold passed reluctantly from the earth, and the retiring fogs revealed an army…”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “As the landscape changed from brown to green, the army awakened, and began to tremble….”

Stylistic Effect

Through intransitive standing-up, relative where-working background, present perfect sensing, existential there-is announcement, appositive buckle-of-noise synesthesia, and coordinate tall-cypresses-swaying, Ondaatje creates syntax of attentive stillness—grammar that moves from human rising and sensing to environmental response, connecting character to landscape through accumulated sensory details.

Information flow: The opening establishes “She stands up in the garden where she has been working and looks into the distance.”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “There is another gust of wind, a buckle of noise in the air, and the tall cypresses sway..”

Stylistic Effect

Through tautological future negative copula, conditional prepositional absence, negative polarity emphasizing totality, complaining verb, and participial posture, Alcott creates syntax of adolescent discontent—grammar that philosophizes about Christmas's essence while grounding complaint in physical sprawl of youthful protest.

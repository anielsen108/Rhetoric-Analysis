Stylistic Effect

Through claimed forgetting, temporal vagueness, and detailed inventory of modest possessions, Cervantes creates oral storytelling syntax. Form IS the tale-teller—grammar that pretends casualness while precisely cataloguing, that claims forgetting while remembering every detail.

Information flow: The opening establishes “In a village of La Mancha, the name of which I have no desire to call to mind, there…”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “In a village of La Mancha, the name of which I have no desire to call to mind, there….”

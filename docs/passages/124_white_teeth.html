Stylistic Effect

Through universal quantifier, adverbial doubling, coordinate spatial opposition, and plural "histories," Smith creates syntax of postcolonial consciousness—grammar that insists every moment contains multiple, irreconcilable narratives, challenging singular historical truth.

Information flow: The opening establishes “Every moment happens twice: inside and outside, and they are two different histories.”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “Every moment happens twice: inside and outside, and they are two different histories..”

Stylistic Effect

Through deictic here-is announcement, indefinite few-years understatement, prepositional in-the-life-of spatial metaphor, participial born/raised biography, coordinate shuffling metaphor, and adjectival dreary-upstate specification, Proulx creates syntax of minimalist introduction—grammar that reduces life story to bare geographical facts and card-shuffle randomness, establishing protagonist through location rather than character.

Information flow: The opening establishes “Here is an account of a few years in the life of Quoyle, born in Brooklyn and raised in a…”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “Here is an account of a few years in the life of Quoyle, born in Brooklyn and raised in a….”

Stylistic Effect

Through passive unmarried state, prepositional essence, universal quantifier, copulative identity, and parallel similes linking cultural and natural essences, James creates syntax of ontological categorization—grammar that makes Olive's spinsterhood as inevitable and essential as Shelley's lyricism or August's heat.

Information flow: The opening establishes “Olive Chancellor was unmarried by every implication of her being.”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “She was a spinster as Shelley was a lyric poet, or as the month of August is sultry..”

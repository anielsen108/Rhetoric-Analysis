Stylistic Effect

Through learning infinitive, battlefield metaphor, conditional concession, and paradoxical causal clause (either BECAUSE both), Stevenson creates syntax of psychological division—grammar that performs the impossibility of unified identity when consciousness contains irreconcilable natures.

Information flow: The opening establishes “I learned to recognize the thorough and primitive duality of man; I saw that, of the two…”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “I learned to recognize the thorough and primitive duality of man; I saw that, of the two….”

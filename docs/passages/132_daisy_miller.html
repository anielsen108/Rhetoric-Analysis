Stylistic Effect

Through hedged gratitude, gerund of formulaic discovery, copulative reduction of person to type, em-dash refinement, and euphemistic abstraction, James creates syntax of categorizing consciousness—grammar showing how Winterbourne's need to classify prevents genuine human connection.

Information flow: The opening establishes “Winterbourne was almost grateful for having found the formula that applied to Miss Daisy…”; subsequent material converts that initial footing into the new emphasis carried by “He had never, as yet, had any relations with young ladies of this category..”

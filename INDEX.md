## Methodology

Each analysis includes:
1. **Author and Source**: Full bibliographic information
2. **Passage**: Complete text being analyzed
3. **Rhetorical Tropes**: Named devices with pronunciations and effects
4. **Syntax as Style Analysis**: Following Virginia Tufte's methodology

- Anaphora, Epistrophe, Epizeuxis
- Metaphor, Simile, Personification
- Chiasmus, Antithesis, Paradox
- Hyperbole, Litotes, Understatement
- Synecdoche, Metonymy
- And 50+ additional classical and modern rhetorical devices
